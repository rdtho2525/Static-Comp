Meet Our Staff

  • Michael Bluth

    Years at the Company: 30 years

    As a child, Michael worked at Bluth's Original Frozen Banana Stand. Either in college or shortly after graduating, Michael began working for his father at the Bluth Company. He put in an honest day's work and never stole from the company, unlike the rest of his family. His father was impressed by his work ethic but he constantly shot his son down to keep working hard for his approval.

  • George Bluth Sr.

    Years at the Company: 68 years

    George Bluth founded the Bluth Company in 1953 and proceeded to grow it into a real estate powerhouse in Newport Beach, California. He didn't do so entirely legally or morally, and in his 50 years as CEO and president George fired his identical twin brother Oscar, systematically replaced the entire staff with younger, cheaper employees, and worked with Saddam Hussein for a quick buck.

    Years at the Company: N/A

She is also mother-in-law to both Tobias Fünke and the deceased Tracey Bluth. The Bluth family as we know it began when George met Lucille, who was working at a Stuckeys.

    Years at the Company: N/A

He tried to do odd jobs for the Bluth Company including copy room work and construction, but he didn't find real employment until he was signed up for Army by his mother on a dare by a Michael Moore impostor.
